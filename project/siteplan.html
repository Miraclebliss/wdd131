Essence of Comfort

A fragrance and body care brand

Site Name

Essence of Comfort — This name reflects a luxurious and soothing fragrance brand that focuses on self-care, emotional wellness, and indulgent scent experiences.

Optional domain: essenceofcomfort.com (availability not required)

Site Purpose

The site serves as a digital home for the “Essence of Comfort” brand. It provides a product catalog featuring oil perfumes, body sprays, and scent-based wellness items, as well as an introduction to the brand and a contact/order form. JavaScript will enhance interactivity for scent filtering and user engagement.

Scenarios

  • What fragrance would you recommend for calming stress or anxiety?
  • Where can I place an order or reach out to customer service?

Color Scheme

  • Soft Lavender (#CBAACB): Used for headers, brand accents, and buttons.
  • Creamy Beige (#F7EFE5): Background color for a calm, inviting look.

Typography

  • Playfair Display – For main headings (elegant and modern)
  • Open Sans – For body text (clean and readable)
